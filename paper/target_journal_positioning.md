# Target Journal Positioning

## Briefings in Bioinformatics

Primary target. Position MolTrustBench as a reproducible bioinformatics resource and reporting standard for auditing molecular AI benchmarks under public exposure and temporal shift. This matches the journal scope around databases, analytical tools, AI/ML in biomedicine, reproducibility, and open science.

## Patterns

Position as a data-science framework for trustworthy benchmark evaluation under public data exposure, with molecular AI as a high-stakes case study.

## Archives of Toxicology

Position through ADMET/Tox benchmark trust, assay provenance, label conflict, and computational toxicology reliability. This becomes stronger after the assay-provenance module is complete.

## Novelty Boundary

MolTrustBench should not compete with ADMET reliability or molecular OOD leaderboards. Its contribution is benchmark temporal validity: whether benchmark molecules, scaffolds, close neighbors, or assay labels were publicly observable before a cutoff used for model pretraining or evaluation.
