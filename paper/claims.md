# MolTrustBench Claims

Evidence status values: `missing`, `preliminary`, `supported`, `strong`.

| Claim | Statement | Status | Current evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| C1 | Molecular AI benchmarks contain measurable public-exposure risk at the molecule, scaffold, and nearest-neighbor levels. | preliminary | Milestone 1 pipeline generates exact, scaffold, and NN exposure annotations plus `results/tables/exposure_summary.csv`. |
| C2 | Exact molecule exposure is only the easiest case; scaffold and chemical-neighbor exposure reveal broader benchmark time-travel risk. | preliminary | Scaffold and NN exposure columns are implemented; full support depends on real ChEMBL release index outputs. |
| C3 | Model performance on standard splits can differ from clean, exposure-removed, and temporal-future subsets. | preliminary | Standard and exact-removed split metrics are generated in `results/tables/slice_metrics.csv`. |
| C4 | Assay provenance and label conflicts are underreported sources of benchmark unreliability, especially for ADMET/Tox endpoints. | missing | Assay-provenance interface exists; extraction and conflict maps are later-phase work. |
| C5 | MolTrustBench provides reusable benchmark trust cards, exposure-adjusted scores, and reporting standards for molecular AI. | preliminary | Trust cards, sanity reports, and `paper/auto_report.md` are generated by the reporting pipeline. |
